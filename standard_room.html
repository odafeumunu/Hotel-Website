DESCRIPTION

The Standard Room at Alarco Hotels and Suites is designed to offer guests a comfortable and welcoming space during their stay. Located in the heart of the city, the Standard Room combines modern amenities with tasteful decor, ensuring a relaxing retreat for both business and leisure travelers.

Upon entering the Standard Room, guests are greeted with a sense of warmth and sophistication. The room features contemporary furnishings, soft lighting, and soothing colors, creating an inviting atmosphere for relaxation. The layout is thoughtfully designed to maximize space and functionality, providing guests with everything they need for a comfortable stay.

Each Standard Room is equipped with a plush queen or king-sized bed, dressed in luxurious linens and pillows, guaranteeing a restful night's sleep. For added convenience, the room includes a spacious work desk and ergonomic chair, ideal for business travelers needing to catch up on work or stay connected with loved ones using the complimentary high-speed internet access.

The en-suite bathroom is elegantly appointed with modern fixtures and amenities, including a walk-in shower, luxurious bathrobe, and premium toiletries, allowing guests to unwind and rejuvenate after a long day of sightseeing or meetings.

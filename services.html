Bush Bar

Sip, Socialize, and Snooker at our Bush Bar

Escape the ordinary and unwind in the rustic charm of our Bush Bar, nestled amidst lush greenery and offering panoramic views. Here, camaraderie meets leisure as you sip on handcrafted cocktails, savor local brews, and indulge in tantalizing snacks. Challenge your companions to a friendly game of snooker, adding excitement to your evening rendezvous. Our uniquely positioned Bush Bar offers maximum relaxation while serving exotic tropical dishes such as suya, barbeque, isi ewu, and ukwobi. Enjoy Chef's Alarco Bites including spiced snails, Buffalo wings, and more, all accompanied by wide-screen television sets for your viewing comfort.
